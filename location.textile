h1. Regular Locations 

The regular location is ThoughtWorks' office in North Dallas. Specifically, that is at 15455 Dallas Parkway, Dallas, TX 75001, on the sixth floor.

There are two parking lots. For both, there is an Arapaho Road exit that is the best way in.  Turn immediately right, go 40 yards and park outside the main lobby entrance if there is room.  There's also a large parking garage that straight ahead as you leave Arapaho road.
